Shrimp n Grits

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Bacon
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Grits
  • Milk
  • Bayleaf

Steps

    First sautee your bacon in a large skillet.

    Then after bacon has been sauteed remove and set aside. Then add the shrimp into the pan with buttter and cajun seasoning.

    After sauteeing the shrimp add garlic into the pan as well.

    In a seprate sauce pan add milk, bayleaf, and grits stir untill the grit absorb all of the milk.

    On a plate add grits to the bottom then add bacon and shrimp to the top of it.
